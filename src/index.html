Cool stuff I've worked on

Technica

Holding one of the largest hackathons in a pandemic is tough! I helped build Technica's online platform for the 2020 season. It supported over 3,000 hackers all over the world for a whole weekend.

Chansey

Chansey aims to prevent large-scale outbreaks on university campuses. Users report their symptoms and give doctors their anonymized data so for analytics. Built at PennApps 2019!

Moody

Moody's goal is to help people with mental health issues. It uses a custom CNN to recognize the user's current mood and logs it. Users can track their mood over time and improve their mental health. Won HackRU Spring 2019!

Intel ISEF 2018

I explored machine learning techniques with my friend as my senior year science project. Namely, the performance of KNN, SVMs, and DNNs, with/without PCA. Our project won the Synopsys science fair and we went to Intel ISEF 2018.
